5 Years from now

To be honest I do have some goals not mentioned in this page like translating a text from a foreign language to English because I cant understand it. The thought only came due to inconvenience using google translate with its translated text incomprehensible. Did I pursue it? No I didn't though it would be great if I did because challenging myself would be healthier for me. As this goal are one of many that I have left abandoned, unfinished, or not yet started.

The mindset that limits my capability "where things get harder just quit" is affecting my judgement and my daily life for years. Can't even have fun or a time to relax because "I need to do something or else the worst case scenarios will happen" thought is haunting me. A coping mechanism that is robbing my life and my future.

So I want to challenge myself and know myself better of how far I am willing, how long can I exposed stressful situations (cant avoid it in my entirety of my life). Want to know if I am capable of understand and doing it. Besides, it will be flexible and scalable to adjust in my mind in order to enter the I.T industry.

As I desire to be someone who is free from my limitation who no longer fears the regret I have committed from my inaction.
